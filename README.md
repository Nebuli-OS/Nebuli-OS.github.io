## BYOD
1. Go to [FreeDNS](https://freedns.afraid.org/)

2. Make an account, you can use an account from a [temporary email provider](https://temp-mail.org/)

3. Once your logged into your account, Go the the [FreeDNS Registary](https://freedns.afraid.org/domain/registry/)

4. Once you find a domain that you want to use, choose your subdomain name, and set the IP as `No IP right now 😭`

5. Finish the captcha and save, then check in 5-60 minutes for your link!
